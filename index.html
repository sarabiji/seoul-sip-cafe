About

This virtual café is a personal project inspired by the ambiance and productivity of real Korean cafés. It is designed to help you relax, focus, and enjoy a cozy virtual environment with customizable sounds and music.

Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important. This website does not collect personal data or track you. All your settings and to-do lists are stored locally in your browser and never sent to any server.
